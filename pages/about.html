Our Story

Miami Walking Tours was founded in 2018 by Carlos Rodriguez, a third-generation Miamian whose family fled Cuba in the 1960s. Growing up in Little Havana, Carlos witnessed the neighborhood's transformation while seeing many tourists pass through without truly experiencing its rich culture.

"I wanted to create an experience that goes beyond the typical tourist traps," Carlos explains. "Our tours are designed to introduce visitors to the real Little Havana—the family-owned cafes where abuelos play dominoes, the cigar factories where master torcedores still roll by hand, the murals that tell stories of exile and hope."

What started as weekend walks with friends has grown into Miami's most highly-rated cultural tour company. Today, our team of 12 local guides has welcomed over 50,000 guests from more than 80 countries, all eager to experience the authentic Miami that locals know and love.
