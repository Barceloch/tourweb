Little Havana Walking Tour

Experience the heart and soul of Cuban Miami

About This Tour

Immerse yourself in the vibrant streets of Little Havana, Miami's most iconic Cuban neighborhood. Our expert local guides will lead you through Calle Ocho's colorful streets, sharing stories of exile and resilience while introducing you to the flavors, sounds, and traditions that make this neighborhood unique.

From the moment you arrive, you'll be treated like family. Visit family-owned businesses that have been serving the community for generations, taste authentic Cuban cuisine at hidden gems known only to locals, and experience the passionate culture that defines Little Havana.

Tour Stops

  1. Start: Cubaocho Museum & Performing Arts Center
  2. Stop 2: Azucar Ice Cream Company
  3. Stop 3: Versailles Restaurant (exterior) & La Carreta
  4. Stop 4: Bay of Pigs Museum
  5. Stop 5: Domino Park (Maximo Gomez Park)
  6. Stop 6: Guayabera Shirt Store
  7. Stop 7: Local cigar factory
  8. End: Calle Ocho Walk of Fame

Meeting Point

Cubaocho Museum & Performing Arts Center
1465 SW 8th Street, Miami, FL 33135
Look for your guide holding a "Miami Walking Tours" sign in front of the main entrance.

Parking available nearby. Public transportation accessible via Metrorail (Vizcaya Station) + trolley.
